About PlaytestHub

PlaytestHub was created by theTrueMikeBrown because he wanted a website that allowed people to playtest each other's games. The website strives to enable the fair playtesting of board games online, with players that help other players out getting the perk of placing their games higher on the list than other player's games.
If you find any issues with this site, you can log them here.

How to use PlaytestHub

In the upper right you will notice a menu. This is where most actions on the site can be taken. Hovering or longpressing on any icon button will tell you what it does.

To add a game

To add a game to the database, click menu on the game list then 'add game'. Fill out the info, and click the save button in the lower right corner.

To playtest a game

To playtest a game, navigate to the game's page. Download all of the game's assets. Click menu and then click 'playtest'. Wait for it to return you to the game list. Once it is done, you are now playtesting the game.
Print up the parts of the game, play it at least once, and then go to the game list and select 'leave feedback'. Fill out the form as completely as possible, and then click submit at the bottom of the page.
The moderator(s) will review your feedback, and if it is complete enough, they will accept it. If they reject your feedback, you will be notified via a message in the message center, and will be given a chance to correct it. The person that created the game will also be notified, and can accept the feedback if they desire to, however the person that created the game cannot reject feedback for their own game.

Priority and getting your games playtested

Once you have gotten your feedback accepted, you will recieve a point which you can use to raise one of your game's priority. Priority controls where your games appear in the game list, so if your games have higher priority, then they will appear at the top of the list. Select 'add or remove points' from the menu to apply points to your games, or reclaim ponts from your games.
Every time that one of your games are playtested it will lose a point of priority, and fall lower in the search results. In order to keep your game at the top of the list you need to keep playtesting other users games.
